Personal Intro

Thank you for visiting my profile page. I am an Infrastructure Engineer (Networking, compute, virtualization & cloud) with more than 10 years experience in the industry verticals. I started off my career as a network Engineer and grew to the peak of my networking career as an expert level SME in Data Cengter, Service Provider, Enterprise Networking & Security. I presently work as a converged/hyper-converged network expert with deep expertise ranging from Data Center networking, cloud, DevOps, service provider networking, solution design & consulting, Project Management, Enterprise Architecture, and services management.

I have earned some of the key industry class certifications including 3 x CCIE (Data Center, Enterprise & Service Provider) , PMP, TOGAF 9, VMware VCAP- Network Virtualization, AWS Solutions Architect Professional, Dell CI/HCI Specialist among others. My interest shifted recently to the application layer and I found no better way to understand it than to engage in a coding BootCamp offered in Georgia Institutue of Technology Coding BootCamp. My plan is to get deeper into the DevOps space and gain a better understanding of the developer's attitude, mindset and execution strategy to enable me engage better with cross-functional teams I come in contact with daily.


I am not sure I see myself building a career in application development (though it is a lot of fun doing those) but more as a network/ virtualization/ cloud engineer solution provider trying to understand the approach of software developers to problem solving. I want to solve the old problems using better tools. I also want to position myself betterto lead cross-functional teams to deliver quality solutions that have direct impact on end users and customers.

Beyond my relationship with extremely smart machines, I am a proud husband, father and a 'culture enthusiast'...if there is anything like that. It basically means that I am interested in learning from diverse people and the rich evolution of their cultural practises. For some strange reasons, I am just interested in that.

What I like, well, I am an avid traveller and I am always facinated by classics. Music? Country. Yes!, I lean a bit conservative on such when I select my playlist but when my mood does, I listen to everything. I like good wine the gentle breeze from the ocean...

Sports? Swimming, tennis and open air running.
