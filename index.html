Personal Intro

Thank you for visiting my profile page. I will not bore you with the usual 'my name is...bla..bla..bla...'. I will just say I consider myself an infrastructure Engineer (Networking, compute, virtualization & cloud) with strong interest in finding new approach to solving old problems. That is what led me to coding.

I am not sure I see myself as an application developer (though it is a lot of fun doing those) but more as a network/ virtualization/ cloud engineer trying to understand the approach of software developers to problem solving. I will be happy to wrap most monotonous activities in a pleasant GUI at the end of my journey but for now, I am just learning as much as I can.
